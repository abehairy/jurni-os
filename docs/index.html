A dashboard for
your life.

Jurni reads across your conversations, social posts, calendar and photos — and turns them into a living picture of who you care about, what you're building, and how you're changing. All on your Mac. Nothing sent anywhere.

One pipeline, every source

Add a new data source with one row in the registry. The processor picks up people, places, projects, tone and patterns — without a single provider-specific branch. 

CONNECTORS       ──→     INGEST           ──→     PROCESS (LLM)      ──→     LANDSCAPE
claude, chatgpt,         kind, author             entities, tone,            who, what,
x, linkedin,             content_hash             patterns, topics           when, how
facebook, instagram      (local sqlite)           (your own API key)         (your Mac)

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
All data stays in ~/.jurni/ on your Mac. No cloud. No account. No telemetry.

What it gives you

A quiet, intelligent layer on top of everything you already do. No feeds. No streaks. No dopamine hooks.

◎

People you actually care about

Jurni weighs who you talk about in your own words over who passes through your feed. Your cofounder beats a viral influencer by default.

◍

Topics that keep coming back

The things you think about across weeks — not what was trending. See which projects, places, and ideas are growing, stalling, or cooling.

◈

Tone, honestly

How a week felt vs. how you remember it. Emotional arcs and shifts, extracted from your own voice — never from a wellness survey.

◇

Local-first, always

Your data never leaves your machine. LLM calls go direct to your API key. We have no servers, no dashboards, and no way to see anything.

◆

Built to extend

Add a connector in minutes. Jurni's pipeline is data-driven — no special cases, no hardcoded provider logic. See CONTRIBUTING.md.

◊

Open source

MIT-licensed. Fork it. Audit it. Build your own. The best way to trust a local-first app is to be able to read every line.

Your data, your Mac, full stop.

Jurni is local-first by design — not as a feature, as a constraint. If your data needs to touch our servers for anything to work, that's a bug.

No cloud Everything lives in ~/.jurni/ on your machine.
No account No login. No email collected. Ever.
